Environment America

Because the Delaware River is a drinking water source for millions of Americans, Environment America contracted me to visualize combined sewer overflows (CSO) in greater Philadelphia. After writing Python code to clean the data, I calculated various discharged statistics. Finally, I mapped the results based on client requests and overlaid the results with recreational areas of concern to local stakeholders.
